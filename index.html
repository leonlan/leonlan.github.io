Leon Lan

Hi, my name is Leon! I am a second-year PhD candidate at the Department of Mathematics of VU Amsterdam. My research interests include production scheduling, vehicle routing problems and metaheuristics.

Projects

Integrating production scheduling and vehicle routing with limited storage
My main PhD project. Together with our industry partners, we are investigating a large-scale optimization problem, consisting of a hybrid flexible flow shop, multi-trip vehicle routing problem and limited intermediate storage.
PyVRP
Open-source, state-of-the-art vehicle routing solver in Python.
VRPLIB
Python library to read and download vehicle routing problem instances.
ALNS
Python implementation of the adaptive large neighborhood search (ALNS) metaheuristic.
EURO-NeurIPS 2022
Competition involving static and dynamic vehicle routing problems with time windows. Our team, OptiML, ranked second place.
