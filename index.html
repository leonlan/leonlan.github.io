Leon Lan

Hey, I'm Leon! I am a third-year PhD candidate at the Department of Mathematics of VU Amsterdam. I work on models and algorithms for real-world production scheduling and vehicle routing problems.

Projects

PyVRP
Open-source, state-of-the-art vehicle routing solver in Python.
VRPLIB
Python library to read and download vehicle routing problem instances.
ALNS
Python implementation of the adaptive large neighborhood search (ALNS) metaheuristic.
PyJobShop
Constraint programming for real-world job shop scheduling problems.
